IN THE CUT LANDSCAPES

Established in 2006, In the Cut Landscapes provides expert gardening and consulting services to prestige residential properties around Sydney. We are known for our meticulous attention to detail and for the prosperity we bring to the gardens we service. We have established relationships with a number of Strata group managing properties including Bayview Towers in Elizabeth Bay, Marlborough Hall in Potts Point and Kamilaroi in Darling Point. We understand the need for thorough documentation and open communication when dealing with Strata.

Prior to establishing In the Cut Landscapes, owner Richard "Jimmy" Jones successfully trained for his Landscape Design Diploma at Ryde TAFE while working for The University of Sydney Grounds Department. Seven years of employment by the University of Sydney has given him a thorough working knowledge of the particular requirements of the university grounds. During the last 3 years of his tenure Jimmy acted in the role of area supervisor, managing four people (3 horticulturists and one apprentice) and was responsible for the ongoing maintenance of one quarter of the university grounds.
